About Crime Mapping

Crime mapping is an essential tool for law enforcement agencies to visualize and analyze spatial patterns of criminal activity. This map helps identify:

  • Crime hotspots and clusters
  • Relationships between crime locations and geographical features
  • Distribution of police stations relative to crime areas
  • Areas that may need increased patrol or surveillance
